# Habits

Four small daily habits, tracked on a 30-day grid. Each top-level row in the database is a habit; the rows nested under it are the daily entries that light up the tracker.

<!-- otion:info {"color":"green","icon":"CalendarCheck","text":"**To log a day:** add a row under the habit (it becomes a child entry), set its **date** to today and its **status** to **Done** or **Skipped**. The Tracker view turns those entries into a streak grid for the last 30 days."} -->

## Tips

- Log skipped days too — an honest gray square beats a gap.
- Keep habits small enough to finish on your worst day.
- Review the grid every Sunday and retire habits that have become automatic.
