# World History

HIST 1120, Mondays and Wednesdays. From the Peace of Westphalia to the end of the Cold War, with a focus on primary sources.

## Running themes

- State sovereignty as an idea: born at Westphalia in 1648, stress-tested ever since.
- Industrialization reshaping who fights wars and why.
- The World Wars as one long crisis with a twenty-year intermission.
- Cold War as competition between systems, fought mostly through proxies.
