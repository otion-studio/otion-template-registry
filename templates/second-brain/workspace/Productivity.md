# Productivity

Productivity isn't about doing more things — it's about doing the *right* things with full attention and letting the rest go. Most "productivity" advice optimises the trivial; the notes here are about protecting the few hours that actually move the needle.

The leverage points: defend uninterrupted focus ([Deep work](Deep work.md)), separate the urgent from the important ([The Eisenhower matrix](The Eisenhower matrix.md)), clear tiny tasks before they pile up ([The two-minute rule](The two-minute rule.md)), and remember that a small share of your effort produces most of your results ([The 80/20 principle](The 80-20 principle.md)). When in doubt, [Default to action](Default to action.md).
