# Creativity

Creativity is less a bolt from the blue and more a slow collision of things you already know. New ideas are recombinations — which means the most creative thing you can do is fill your head with diverse material and then give it room to connect.

This cluster traces how that happens: ideas surface from a long, quiet incubation ([Idea emergence](Idea emergence.md)); they're nudged into existence by limits rather than freedom ([Constraints breed creativity](Constraints breed creativity.md)); and each new combination opens the door to the next one ([The adjacent possible](The adjacent possible.md)). The state in which the work flows is its own note: [Flow](Flow.md).
