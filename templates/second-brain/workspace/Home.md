# Home

Welcome to your second brain — a place to keep ideas instead of just collecting them. This is a **Map of Content**: the doorway into a web of small, interlinked notes. You don't navigate it like a filing cabinet, top-down through folders. You navigate it like a city, following links from one idea to the next.

<!-- otion:info {"color":"blue","icon":"Lightbulb","text":"**How this works.** Every note holds one idea and links to its neighbours. When two notes connect, make the link explicit — that link is where new thinking happens. Start anywhere, follow what's interesting, and add a link whenever an idea reminds you of another. See [Atomic notes](Atomic notes.md) and [Evergreen notes](Evergreen notes.md) for the method itself."} -->

The hubs below are themselves Maps of Content. Each gathers the atomic notes in one cluster and points to the others. Nothing here is finished — a second brain is a garden, not a monument.
