# Sources

A second brain is honest about where its ideas come from. Most of the notes here trace back to a handful of books, essays, and talks — listed below so you can follow any idea back to its root and read the original.

A note about reading: the point isn't to finish books, it's to *harvest* them. Read with a pen, pull out the one or two ideas worth keeping, and turn each into an [Atomic notes](Atomic notes.md) entry linked into the web. The book becomes a source; the note becomes yours.
