# Aurora Smart-Home Launch — Project Charter

Aurora is our first consumer smart-home product line: a Matter-certified hub plus a companion app, sold through retail and direct channels. This charter is the single source of truth for what we're building, who's accountable, and how we'll know we succeeded.

<!-- otion:info {"color":"blue","icon":"Target","text":"**Goal.** Ship the Aurora hub and companion app to market by **October 9, 2026**, with 3 national retail partners signed and 20,000 units in the channel at launch."} -->

## Scope

In scope for this initiative:

- The Aurora hub hardware (Matter + Zigbee radio, injection-molded enclosure).
- The companion app for iOS and Android, localized into 6 languages.
- Cloud account-linking and the automations / scenes engine.
- Retail and direct-to-consumer go-to-market, including a pre-order page.

<!-- otion:info {"color":"yellow","icon":"AlertTriangle","text":"**Out of scope.** A voice assistant, third-party developer SDK, and subscription services are explicitly deferred to a fast-follow release in 2027."} -->
