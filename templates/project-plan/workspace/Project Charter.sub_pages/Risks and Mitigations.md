## How we score

- **Likelihood** — Low / Medium / High, based on how probable the risk is this quarter.
- **Impact** — Low / Medium / High, based on the effect on date, budget, or quality if it lands.
- A **High / High** risk is escalated to the sponsor immediately and gets a named mitigation with a deadline.

## Mitigation notes

- **Matter certification slip** — we booked the certification lab slot early and are running pre-certification in-house first.
- **Single-source Wi-Fi module** — Operations is qualifying a second supplier in parallel as a fallback.
- **Support under-staffed for launch week** — People is standing up a temporary war room and a surge-staffing plan.
