# Stakeholders

Who has skin in the Aurora launch, and what each of them needs from us. We map every stakeholder to a RACI level so sign-offs never stall on "who decides".

<!-- otion:info {"color":"purple","icon":"Info","text":"**RACI** = Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed. Every phase gate needs the Accountable stakeholder's explicit sign-off before we proceed."} -->

## Executive sponsor

- **Marcus Feld — SVP, Consumer Products.** Accountable for the launch P&L. Approves the budget and the go / no-go at each gate. Wants a one-page status every Friday.

## Steering committee

- **Priya Raman — Program Lead.** Responsible for delivery across all phases. Owns this charter.
- **Mei-Ling Chen — Director of Engineering.** Accountable for build and certification.
- **Daniel Okafor — Head of Product Marketing.** Accountable for go-to-market.
- **Hannah Bergström — Finance Business Partner.** Consulted on pricing, margin, and spend.
